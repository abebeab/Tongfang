Overview

Precision thermal energy measurement for heating and cooling applications

Our BTU meters provide accurate measurement of thermal energy in hydronic heating and cooling systems. By measuring flow rate and temperature differential, they calculate energy consumption for billing, benchmarking, and optimization. Available with pulse output, MODBUS, and BACnet communication protocols, they integrate seamlessly with the EMC-2000 Energy Metering System.

Overview

Comprehensive power metering solutions for energy monitoring and management

Our power meters provide accurate energy measurement for commercial and industrial applications. Available with BACnet and MODBUS communication protocols, they integrate seamlessly with building management systems for real-time energy monitoring, sub-metering, and demand management. From compact DIN-rail meters to advanced power controllers, we have solutions for every application.

BACnet MS/TP - Native BMS Integration
MODBUS RTU - Industrial Communication
Class 0.5 Accuracy - Precision Measurement

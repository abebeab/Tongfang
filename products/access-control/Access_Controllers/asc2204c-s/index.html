ASC2204C-S

Dahua 4-Door Two‑way Access Controller

Key Features

  • Web‑Based 4 Door 2 Way Networked Access Controller
  • 100,000 Users, 100,000 Cards, 3,000 Fingerprints, 500,000 Records
  • 5 x RS485 ports, 4 x Wiegand ports
  • 6 Alarm Inputs (digital), 4 Alarm Outputs (relay)
  • Supports Anti-Passback, Multi-Person Unlock, First-Card Unlock, Remote Verification
  • Backup DC12V7A battery support & fire alarm linkage
  • SECC steel casing, IP20 / IK04 protection

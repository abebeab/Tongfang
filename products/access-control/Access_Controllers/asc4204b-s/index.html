ASC4204B-S

Dahua Four Door One‑way Access Controller

Key Features

  • Web‑Based 4 Door 1 Way Networked Access Controller
  • 100,000 Users, 100,000 Cards, 10,000 Fingerprints, 500,000 Records
  • 5 x RS485 ports, 4 x Wiegand ports
  • 1 Alarm Input, 1 Alarm Output – expandable up to 8 each
  • Supplies power to 4 locks and 4 card readers
  • Anti‑Passback, Multi‑Person Unlock, First‑Card Unlock, Remote Verification
  • Fire alarm linkage support
  • DC12V / PoE power supply options

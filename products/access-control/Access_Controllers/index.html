Smart Door Access Control System

Our access control system is an advanced and comprehensive solution designed to enhance security and control access at a variety of locations. The Access Controller can be integrated with various other devices, such as card readers, fingerprint readers, locks or push buttons, to satisfy any user's unique requirements.

Enhanced Security

Multi-layer authentication with advanced encryption protocols

Easy Configuration

Web or unified platform management for simple operation

Versatile Applications

Access Controllers

Pro Series

Provide the highest level of security and functionality for enterprise applications

Ultra Series

Designed for subways, airports, banks, and high-security government facilities

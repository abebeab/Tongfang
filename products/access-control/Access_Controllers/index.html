Smart Door Access Control System

Our access control system is an advanced and comprehensive solution designed to enhance security and control access at a variety of locations. The Access Controller can be integrated with various other devices, such as card readers, fingerprint readers, locks or push buttons, to satisfy any user's unique requirements.

Enhanced Security

Multi-layer authentication with advanced encryption protocols

Easy Configuration

Web management or unified platform management for simple operation

Versatile Applications

Commercial, industrial, or residential settings - reliable and versatile

Product Categories

Our access controllers are available in different series - Value, Pro, and Ultra - suitable for a wide range of scenarios with multiple application methods.

Pro Series

Provide the highest level of security and functionality

Ultra Series

Designed for subways, airports, banks, and more

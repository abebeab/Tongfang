ASR2101A

Advanced Access Reader (Card + Password)

Key Features

  • PC material with acrylic panel, IP66 protection for indoor/outdoor use
  • Contactless recognition of IC Card (Mifare Classic 1K, 13.56MHz)
  • Card swiping and password unlock modes (multiple unlocking modes)
  • RS-485 and Wiegand 34 communication protocols (Wiegand 26 configurable)
  • Buzzer and indicator light prompts
  • Anti-tampering alarm for enhanced security
  • Built-in watchdog for abnormal operation detection and recovery
  • Overcurrent and overvoltage protection on all connection ports

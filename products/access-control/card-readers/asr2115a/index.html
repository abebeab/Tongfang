ASR2115A

Waterproof QR Code Reader (IC+Password+QR Code)

Key Features

  • Water-proof QR Code, Card and Password Reader
  • RS-485 and Wiegand (W26/W34) Protocol
  • Mifare Classic 1K Card (13.56MHz) Reading
  • Mechanical Keypad for Operation
  • Prompts Using The Buzzer and Indicator Light
  • IP66 Protection Rating

Precision Logic. Global Interoperability.

At Tongfang, we integrate the Hysine BCU Series to create the "Logic Backbone" of your facility. These are not just controllers; they are 32-bit ARM-based intelligence nodes designed for 100ms real-time response in airports, hospitals, and high-end industrial parks.

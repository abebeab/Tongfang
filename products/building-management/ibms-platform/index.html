System Architecture

Centralized control for all building systems

Our Integrated Building Management Systems (IBMS) provide centralized control and monitoring for all building systems. From the 3D BIM-based BIMview platform to the comprehensive InteServ integration platform, our solutions connect building automation, energy management, security, fire, and IoT systems into a single intelligent management center.

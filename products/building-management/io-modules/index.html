Overview

Expand your DDC controller capabilities with modular I/O solutions

Our I/O modules provide flexible expansion for DDC controllers in building automation systems. Available in various configurations including universal inputs, digital outputs, analog outputs, and high-density modules, they allow you to customize your control system for any application size and complexity.

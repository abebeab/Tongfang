Industrial-Grade Control Panel Solutions

Tongfang control panels provide centralized command and monitoring for building automation systems. From MCCs to custom PLC panels, we design and fabricate robust solutions that meet international standards for safety, reliability, and performance.

Custom Engineering

Tailored panel designs based on project-specific requirements and equipment specifications.

UL & IEC Certified

Precision Energy Measurement Solutions

Tongfang energy meters provide accurate measurement and sub-metering of electrical power, thermal energy, water, and gas consumption for building performance analysis, cost allocation, and sustainability reporting.

Class 0.5S Accuracy

High-precision measurement compliant with IEC 62053-22 for billing-grade accuracy.

Multi-Protocol Support

Simultaneous communication via Modbus, BACnet, M-Bus, and LoRaWAN for flexible integration.

The FW2561-RI Remote Indicator is a critical visual notification component for modern fire safety systems. Part of the Maple Armor 2025 series, it provides clear visual status indication for remote locations, ensuring immediate awareness of fire alarm system status in complex building environments.

  • Multiple status indications (Normal, Alarm, Fault)

Product Overview

The FW2561-RI Remote Indicator provides clear visual status indication for fire alarm systems in remote or critical locations. Designed for reliability and easy integration, it enhances safety awareness by providing immediate visual feedback on system status.

Advanced Features:
  • Multi-Color LED Indicators: Green for normal operation, Red for alarm, Yellow for fault conditions
  • High Visibility: 180° viewing angle ensures visibility from multiple directions
  • Low Power Consumption: Energy-efficient design minimizes system load
  • Easy Installation: Simple wiring and mounting for quick deployment
  • Durable Construction: Designed for reliable operation in various environments
  • System Integration: Seamlessly integrates with Maple Armor fire alarm control panels
Applications:
Commercial Buildings

Lobbies, corridors, and remote areas requiring visual status indication.

Healthcare Facilities

Nurse stations, waiting areas, and critical care locations.

Industrial Facilities

Control rooms, equipment areas, and remote monitoring points.

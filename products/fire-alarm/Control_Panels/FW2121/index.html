Product Overview

The FW2121 (Remote LCD Annunciator) provides industry-leading fire detection and notification capabilities. It is fully compatible with Tongfang BMS architecture and optimized for quick field deployment and simplified maintenance.

High-Resolution Display

7" color TFT LCD with excellent readability from multiple viewing angles in various lighting conditions.

Multi-Panel Monitoring

Monitor up to 250 fire alarm control panels from a single display location across the network.

Priority Alerting

Visual and audible alerts with priority-based notification (Alarm > Trouble > Supervisory).

Network Capabilities

Installation Guidelines
  • Mount at eye level (48-60 inches from floor)
  • Ensure adequate viewing distance (3-10 feet)
  • Avoid direct sunlight on display
  • Provide proper ventilation around unit
  • Use included cable strain relief
Wiring Requirements

Network Wiring

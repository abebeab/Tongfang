Product Overview

The FW2512 Conventional Smoke Detector is a photoelectric smoke detector designed for reliable fire detection in conventional (non-addressable) fire alarm systems. It utilizes advanced optical sensing technology to detect visible smoke particles typical of smoldering fires.

Photoelectric Technology

Advanced optical chamber for early detection of smoldering fires.

Dual Wiring Options

Configurable for both 2-wire and 4-wire conventional systems.

Easy Testing

Built-in magnetic test capability for simplified functional testing.

Key Features

  • Photoelectric smoke detection technology
  • 2-wire or 4-wire selectable configuration
  • Magnetic test capability
  • 360° visible alarm LED
  • Automatic drift compensation
  • Low power consumption

Product Overview

The FW562 Duct Smoke Detector is a UL listed smoke detection device specifically engineered for HVAC duct systems. It provides early detection of smoke spreading through ventilation ducts, helping to prevent smoke circulation throughout buildings during fire events. Utilizing advanced photoelectric technology, it samples air from the duct and provides reliable detection even in challenging airflow conditions.

UL Listed

Meets rigorous UL standards for duct smoke detector performance and reliability.

Airflow Compensation

Automatic compensation for varying duct air velocities (100-4,000 FPM).

HVAC Optimized

Specifically designed for duct mounting and HVAC system integration.

Detection Technology
Air Sampling

Continuously samples air from HVAC ducts using precision sampling tubes.

Monitors and compensates for duct air velocity variations.

Installation Guidelines
  • Install per NFPA 90A requirements
  • Mount on straight duct sections (min 5 duct diameters from bends)
  • Position sampling tubes perpendicular to airflow
  • Ensure proper tube length (protrude 1/3 into duct)
  • Provide access for testing and maintenance
Wiring Requirements
Power Requirements

12-30VDC, 50μA standby, 60mA alarm. Use fire-rated cable.

Wiring Options

2-wire (power/alarm) or 4-wire (power, alarm, relay, remote).

Terminal Connections

Screw terminals accept 12-22 AWG wire. Clearly marked terminals.

Installation Steps
  1. Turn off power to fire alarm and HVAC systems
  2. Select location per NFPA 90A requirements
  3. Cut mounting hole in duct (size per instructions)
  4. Install sampling tube mounting collars
  5. Mount detector controller adjacent to duct
  6. Connect sampling tubes to controller
  7. Connect wiring (power, alarm, relay if used)
  8. Restore power to systems
  9. Test detector using magnet or remote test
  10. Verify airflow and detection operation

The FW2722 Two-Stage Station is an advanced manual fire alarm call point designed for facilities requiring controlled emergency response. This station provides sequential activation capabilities - first initiating an alert stage for investigation, followed by a full alarm stage for complete evacuation when necessary. Ideal for hospitals, airports, industrial facilities, and other complex environments.

Product Overview

The FW2722 Two-Stage Station represents an advanced approach to manual fire alarm initiation, designed for facilities where controlled emergency response is critical. By providing sequential activation capabilities, this device allows for a measured response to potential emergencies - first notifying designated personnel for investigation while avoiding immediate full-building evacuation, then escalating to complete alarm if the situation requires it.

UL 38 Staged Activation

Certified to UL Standard 38 for Manual Fire Alarm Boxes.

Controlled Response

Allows investigation before full evacuation.

Operational Continuity

Minimizes disruption to critical operations.

Key Features

  • Two-stage sequential activation (Alert → Alarm)
  • Configurable stage timing (30s to 3min)
  • Automatic escalation from alert to alarm
  • Dual-color LED stage indicators (Yellow/Red)
  • Key-operated reset mechanism
  • IK08 impact resistance rating

Installation Guidelines

  • Mount 48" above finished floor (ADA recommendation)
  • Install in designated emergency response areas
  • Use 24VDC system voltage, dual normally open contacts
  • Four-wire connection for stage separation
  • Screw terminals accept 12-22 AWG wire

Ideal Applications

  • Hospitals and healthcare facilities
  • Airports and transportation hubs
  • Industrial manufacturing plants
  • Data centers and server rooms
  • Research laboratories

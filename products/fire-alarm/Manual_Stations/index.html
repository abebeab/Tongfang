Manual Station Selection Guide

Choose the right manual station for your application:

  • FW2721 Dual Action – Standard applications, prevents accidental activation
  • FW2722 Two-Stage – Areas requiring deliberate two-step activation
  • FW2723 Single Action – Quick activation areas, compatible with FW2105
  • FW2731/FW2732 – Special hazard areas with agent release systems

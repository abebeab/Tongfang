Capacitive Touch Technology

The T-7772 uses capacitive touch sensors with no moving parts, ensuring long life and reliable operation. The sleek, flat surface is easy to clean and resists dust and moisture. Touch response is instant and requires only a light touch.

Product Overview – T-7772

The T-7772 is a modern, capacitive touch panel that provides local control of volume, VOD functions, and task status for ITC T-7700 series audio systems. Its sleek design and standard 86-box mounting make it perfect for hotels, conference rooms, classrooms, and residential installations.

Capacitive Touch Interface: Unlike mechanical buttons that can wear out, the T-7772's capacitive touch sensors provide reliable, maintenance-free operation. The smooth glass surface is easy to clean and resistant to moisture.

Volume Control: Adjust volume up/down for the connected zone. Volume level is displayed via LED indicators.

VOD Control: Integrated VOD (Video on Demand) functions allow users to control media playback – play, pause, next, previous – ideal for hotel rooms and residential installations.

Task Status Display: The panel shows the status of scheduled tasks – for example, indicating when a "Do Not Disturb" mode is active or when a wake-up call is scheduled.

Easy Installation: Mounts in any standard 86x86mm electrical box. Connects to the main system via a simple cable (≤15m). Powered directly from the connected device (DC24V).

Easy Integration

The T-7772 connects directly to compatible ITC devices such as T-7705A, T-7707 series, and T-7701 decoders. Configuration is done via T-7700RP software – assign functions, set volume limits, and customize LED behavior. No local programming required.

Cable Length Limitation

Maximum cable length between T-7772 and host device is 15 meters. For longer distances, use a signal booster or locate the host device closer to the panel. The connection uses standard Ethernet cable for RJ45 versions.

Capacitive Touch Advantages

Unlike mechanical buttons, capacitive touch panels have no moving parts – they never wear out. The smooth surface is easy to clean, resists moisture, and provides a modern, premium feel. Perfect for hotels and high-end installations.

Product Overview – T-7782POEH

The T-7782POEH is a specialized outdoor IP speaker designed for the growing smart city market. Its compact form factor, ultra-lightweight construction, and POE power make it the ideal choice for integration with intelligent street lighting, urban IoT infrastructure, and smart city communication networks.

Smart City Ready: As cities become smarter, light poles are evolving into connected hubs for sensors, cameras, and communication devices. The T-7782POEH is designed to be part of this ecosystem – small, lightweight, and powered directly from the pole's POE network connection.

Ultra-Compact & Lightweight: At just 1.45kg and 280mm tall, the T-7782POEH adds minimal weight and wind load to light poles. This makes it suitable for retrofit installations on existing poles as well as new smart city deployments.

25W of Clean Outdoor Audio: With 25W of Class D amplification, the T-7782POEH delivers clear, intelligible audio for public announcements, emergency alerts, background music, and urban communication.

POE-Powered Simplicity: Powered entirely by POE+ (802.3at), the T-7782POEH requires only a single Ethernet cable. No separate power wiring, no electrician, no complex installation – just plug into the light pole's network switch.

IP66 Weatherproof Construction: Like all T-7782 series speakers, the POEH model features IP66-rated aluminum alloy construction, completely dust-tight and protected against powerful water jets – essential for outdoor urban environments.

IP66 – What It Means

IP66 rating: 6 = dust-tight (no dust ingress), 6 = protected against powerful water jets (heavy seas, hose-down). Suitable for outdoor use in all weather conditions, including rain, snow, and cleaning with pressure washers. Not suitable for submersion.

Product Overview – T-7784H

The T-7784H is a specialized outdoor IP column speaker designed for extreme cold environments where standard speakers fail. With an operating range of -35°C to 60°C, IP66 weatherproofing, and 40W of clean audio output, it delivers reliable performance in the world's coldest locations.

Arctic-Grade Engineering: Unlike standard outdoor speakers that may fail below -20°C, the T-7784H incorporates:

  • Low-temperature lubricants for moving parts
  • Arctic-grade rubber seals that remain flexible at -40°C
  • Capacitors and components rated for extended cold operation
  • Special diaphragm material that maintains flexibility in extreme cold

40W High Power for Harsh Environments: With 40W of clean Class D amplification, the T-7784H can overcome wind noise and deliver clear announcements in challenging outdoor conditions.

IP66 Weatherproof: Complete dust protection and resistance to powerful water jets – rain, snow, ice, and hose-down are no problem. The aluminum alloy enclosure resists corrosion and UV degradation.

Solar Compatible: The 24V DC power input allows connection to solar power systems, making the T-7784H ideal for remote off-grid locations such as arctic research stations, mountain tops, and remote industrial sites.

Why Choose T-7784H for Extreme Cold?

Standard outdoor speakers are typically rated to -20°C. Below that, seals become brittle, lubricants freeze, and components fail. The T-7784H is specifically engineered for extreme cold environments:

  • Operating temperature: -35°C to 60°C
  • Arctic-grade seals: Remain flexible at -40°C
  • Low-temperature lubricants: No freezing of moving parts
  • Cold-flex diaphragm: Maintains audio quality in extreme cold
  • Wide-range capacitors: Rated for -40°C operation

If your project involves temperatures below -20°C, the T-7784H is the reliable choice.

IP66 – What It Means

IP66 rating: 6 = dust-tight (no dust ingress), 6 = protected against powerful water jets (heavy seas, hose-down). Suitable for outdoor use in all weather conditions, including rain, snow, ice, and cleaning with pressure washers. Not suitable for submersion.

Installation in Extreme Cold

For installations below -35°C, consider the optional heated enclosure. The T-7784H itself is rated to -35°C, but wind chill and extreme conditions may require additional protection. Solar power systems should use batteries rated for cold temperatures.

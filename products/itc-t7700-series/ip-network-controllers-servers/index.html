All T-7700 series controllers support T-7700SR hot backup software. Deploy in active-standby pairs for 99.999% availability. Automatic failover in under 300 milliseconds – no audio dropout, no lost calls. Ideal for emergency communication, healthcare, and transportation systems.

System Architecture

Centralized control, distributed intelligence.

The T-7700 series controllers serve as the central brain of your IP audio network. They run the T-7700RP management software, maintain the SQL database of terminals and users, process scheduling, and handle fire alarm integration.

Decoders (T-7701, T-7704) and IP amplifiers (T-7700B series) receive streaming audio directly from the controller. Paging microphones (T-7702A/M) and intercom stations (T-7700A/AN) establish real-time full-duplex voice paths.

Redundancy options:

  • T-7700SR: Active-standby server pair
  • T-7700A: Dual NIC failover
  • t-7700h: Hot-swap PSU, RAID, IPMI

Zone Power Allocation

The T-77240Z has a total power budget of 240W shared across eight zones. Power can be allocated dynamically – for example, Zone 1 can use 100W while Zones 2-8 share the remaining 140W, or all zones can be limited to 30W each. Per-zone power limits are configurable via software to prevent overload. Ideal for applications with varying zone size requirements.

Product Overview – T-77240Z

The T-77240Z is a unique 8-zone IP network power amplifier that combines multi-zone routing with built-in amplification. Unlike separate decoder + amplifier solutions, the T-77240Z receives network audio, routes it to any of eight independent 100V zones, and amplifies it internally – all in a single 2U rack chassis.

Complete Zone Amplification: Each of the eight zones is a dedicated 100V constant voltage output with its own amplifier stage. Zones can be activated individually, in groups, or all simultaneously. This eliminates the need for external amplifiers and complex wiring, significantly reducing installation time and cost.

Flexible Power Distribution: With 240W total power, the T-77240Z can drive a mix of small and large zones. A school might use 30W for each of 8 classroom wings (240W total), while a retail store might allocate 100W to the main sales floor and 20W each to seven smaller areas. Power limits are software-configurable per zone.

Fire Alarm Integration: Dedicated fire alarm linkage inputs allow emergency messages to override any zone with highest priority. Programmable evacuation patterns (e.g., alternate zone activation) comply with international life safety standards.

Local Audio Inputs: Front/rear MIC and AUX inputs allow local announcement capability without network dependency. The EMC input provides absolute priority override for emergency paging.

Intelligent Power Management

Each zone has independent power management. Zones enter standby (near-zero power consumption) when no audio is present for a configurable period (5-60 minutes). Zones power up instantly (within 0.5 seconds) when audio is detected. This can reduce overall power consumption by up to 70% in multi-zone installations.

Power Allocation Warning

The T-77240Z has a total power budget of 240W shared across 8 zones. Ensure total speaker wattage connected to all zones does not exceed 240W. Use the T-7700RP software to set per-zone power limits and prevent overload. Example: 8 zones × 30W speakers = 240W total.

Zone Grouping

Zones can be logically grouped in the T-7700RP software. For example, create an "All Call" group that activates zones 1-8 simultaneously, or "East Wing" group with zones 1,2,3. Group paging can be triggered from any T-7702A/M paging microphone with a single button press.

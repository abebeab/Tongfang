Product Overview – T-100PT

The T-100PT is a rugged, intelligent portable terminal designed for field operations where durability and connectivity are essential. Combining 4G/WiFi communication, GPS tracking, and a 500W camera, it's the ideal tool for patrol inspection, field service, and mobile workforce management.

Always Connected: With 4G LTE and WiFi connectivity, the T-100PT keeps field personnel in constant communication with the control center. VPN support ensures secure data transmission over public networks.

GPS Tracking & Location Services: Built-in GPS allows real-time tracking of personnel and assets. Ideal for security patrols, delivery verification, and field service management.

Capture Evidence & Documentation: The 500W camera enables photo documentation of incidents, inspections, and field conditions. The built-in flashlight provides illumination in low-light environments.

Rugged & Reliable: Anti-pry, anti-drop construction ensures the device survives the rigors of field use. The 1600mAh battery provides all-day operation on a single charge.

Customizable Applications: The Android-based operating system can run custom inspection apps, patrol logging software, and field service tools. Integration with the T-7700 server enables real-time data synchronization.

Anti-Pry & Anti-Drop Design

The T-100PT is engineered for field use where consumer devices fail. Anti-pry construction prevents tampering, while anti-drop design withstands falls from 1.2m onto concrete. IP54 splash resistance protects against rain and spills.

GPS Patrol Tracking

Integrate with T-7700RP software for real-time patrol tracking. Dispatcher can view all patrol units on a map, receive alerts, and dispatch assistance. Historical patrol routes can be reviewed for compliance.

Typical Deployment – Security Patrol

T-100PT units issued to security guards with T-7700 server at control center

Guards carry T-100PT on patrol. GPS tracks their location in real-time. At checkpoints, they scan QR codes and take photos. Any incidents are reported immediately with photo evidence. Control center dispatches backup when needed. All data syncs to server for compliance records.

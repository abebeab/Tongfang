2x30W High-Power Amplifier

The T-7707M features a 2x30W digital amplifier – double the power of standard T-7707 models. Ideal for larger classrooms, training rooms, and spaces requiring higher volume levels or driving less efficient speakers. The amplifier delivers clean, distortion-free audio even at maximum output.

Product Overview – T-7707M

The T-7707M is a high-power wall-mounted IP active speaker that combines 2x30W of digital amplification with a built-in 2.4GHz wireless microphone. It's designed for applications where standard 15W speakers aren't sufficient and hands-free presentation is required.

High Power for Larger Spaces: With 2x30W of clean digital amplification, the T-7707M can fill larger classrooms, training rooms, and open-plan areas with clear, intelligible audio. Perfect for:

  • Large classrooms and lecture halls
  • Training rooms and conference facilities
  • Retail spaces with high ambient noise
  • Restaurants and cafeterias
  • Anywhere more volume is needed

Built-in 2.4G Wireless Mic: The included handheld microphone (or optional headset/lavalier) provides hands-free operation for teachers, trainers, and presenters. The microphone automatically pairs when powered on within range, with up to 30m range and 8+ hour battery life.

AUX Input for Local Sources: The rear-panel RCA AUX input allows connection of external devices such as media players, TVs, or conference systems. The AUX source can be mixed with network audio or selected as the primary source via T-7700RP software.

Easy Installation: Powered by PoE (802.3af) – a single Ethernet cable provides data and power. For maximum 30W output, the optional 24V DC adapter is recommended.

Power Supply Considerations

The T-7707M's 2x30W amplifier draws more power than standard models. When using PoE (802.3af), available power is approximately 15W per channel – sufficient for most speech and background music. For maximum 30W per channel output, use the optional 24V DC adapter.

System Compatibility – T-7707M

Required Controllers & Software
  • T-7700 / T-7700A / t-7700h – Server hardware
  • T-7700RP / T-7700RU – Broadcasting management software
  • PoE switch (802.3af) or DC 24V power supply
  • No external amplifier required (self-powered)

T-7707M vs T-7707BM

The T-7707M is identical to the T-7707BM but without the MP3 player and USB port. Choose T-7707M when you need high power and wireless mic but don't require local media playback. Choose T-7707BM when you need the MP3 feature.

Power Supply Recommendation

For maximum 30W per channel output, use the optional 24V DC adapter. When using PoE (802.3af), available power is limited to approximately 12-15W per channel due to PoE budget constraints. This is still sufficient for most speech and background music applications.

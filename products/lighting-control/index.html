Architectural Scene Management

"Feed-Through" Smart Relay Logic

Tongfang integrates the Hysine BCX Series to provide industrial-grade lighting control. Designed as a pole contactor equivalent, these DIN-rail modules feature independent intelligence—ensuring that localized schedules and safety logic continue to operate even if the BMS server is offline.

Standalone Real-time Clock
Dual BACnet & Modbus Ports
16A High-Inrush Capability
0~10V Precise LED Dimming

Integrator Advantage

We integrate BCX modules directly into switchboards. The DIN-rail mountable design and "Feed-Through" circuit logic minimize heat and maximize electrical safety in mission-critical facilities.

Precision Senses & Technical Nodes

The "Tail End" is the most critical physical layer of the BMS. Hysine technical sensors provide high-fidelity environmental data directly to the logic layer. From BVC-series duct probes to differential pressure switches, we ensure absolute accuracy for clean rooms, hospitals, and industrial facilities.

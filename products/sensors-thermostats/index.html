Precision Environmental Monitoring

Tongfang sensors and thermostats provide accurate measurement and control of building environments, enabling energy-efficient HVAC operation and optimal indoor air quality.

High Accuracy

±0.3°C temperature accuracy and ±2% RH humidity accuracy for precise environmental control.

Wireless Options

Battery-powered wireless sensors with 5+ year battery life for flexible installation.

Sensor Placement

Proper sensor placement is critical for accurate measurements. Follow our guidelines for optimal results.

  • Keep away from heat sources
  • Ensure proper air circulation
  • Calibrate annually

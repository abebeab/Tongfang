1. Runway Management

Challenges
  • Monitoring landing and takeoff in low visibility (fog/night)
  • Preventing unauthorized incursions on active runways
  • Debris detection over vast distances
  • Coordination between multiple aircraft movements
  • Weather monitoring and hazard detection
Tongfang Solution

Thermal Detection: High-contrast thermal imaging sees aircraft and obstacles clearly in total darkness.

Long Distance Viewing: Powerful optical zoom cameras cover kilometers of runway length.

Panoramic Mode: Stitched 180°/360° views provide situational awareness for ATC.

AI-based Intrusion Detection: Automated alerts for unauthorized runway access.

Key Features:
  • 24/7 Visibility
  • Enhanced Safety
  • Reduced Delays

2. Perimeter Protection

Challenges
  • Extensive boundary lines difficult to patrol manually
  • False alarms caused by wildlife or weather
  • Delayed response times to intrusion events
  • Cost of physical security personnel
  • Regulatory compliance requirements
Tongfang Solution

Radar Linkage: Radar detects movement trajectory and guides PTZ cameras to zoom in automatically.

AI Intrusion Detection: Distinguishes humans/vehicles from animals to reduce false alarms.

Active Deterrence: Automated strobe lights and audio warnings repel intruders instantly.

Multi-layered Security: Fence sensors, CCTV, and access control integration.

Key Features:
  • 95% False Alarm Reduction
  • Instant Response
  • 40% Cost Savings

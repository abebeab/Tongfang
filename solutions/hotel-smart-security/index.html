1. Smart Parking Lot Management

Challenges

Hotel parking facilities often face congestion, inefficient space utilization, security concerns, and high energy consumption from 24/7 lighting. Guests struggle to find available spaces, leading to frustration and potential safety issues.

Traditional parking systems lack real-time monitoring, automated guidance, and energy-efficient operations, resulting in poor guest experiences and increased operational costs.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides intelligent parking solutions with real-time space detection, automated guidance systems, energy-efficient lighting control, and integrated security monitoring for seamless guest parking experiences.

Real-time Space Detection

Ultrasonic sensors and camera systems detect available parking spaces. Real-time updates displayed on digital signage throughout the parking area.

Automated Guidance Systems

LED guidance lights direct guests to available spaces. Mobile app integration for pre-booking and navigation. License plate recognition for automated entry/exit.

Benefits

  • Increase parking space utilization by 30-40%
  • Reduce guest parking time by 50%
  • Lower energy costs with smart lighting control
  • Enhance security with comprehensive monitoring
  • Improve guest satisfaction with hassle-free parking

Key Features

Smart Lighting Control

Automated vehicle identification and access control

Continuous surveillance and occupancy tracking

Video Analytics & Monitoring

Benefits

1. Production Safety & Monitoring

Challenges
  • Manual safety checks are prone to error.
  • Equipment overheating causes unexpected downtime.
  • Difficulty enforcing PPE (helmet/vest) compliance.
Tongfang Solution

AI Safety Monitoring: Cameras automatically detect missing helmets or vests and trigger alarms.

Thermal Inspection: Identify overheating machinery before failure occurs.

Process Visualization: Integrate SCADA data with video for visual verification of alarms.

2. Smart Warehouse & Logistics

Challenges

Inventory theft, fire risks in large storage areas, and inefficient loading dock management leading to delays.

Tongfang Solution

Early Fire Detection: Thermal cameras and aspirating smoke detectors identify risks seconds after ignition.

ANPR Dock Management: Automatically recognize delivery trucks and assign dock bays.

Asset Tracking: RFID integration to track high-value raw materials.

3. Perimeter Protection

Challenges

Large industrial parks have vast perimeters that are difficult to patrol manually, especially at night or in bad weather.

Tongfang Solution

Radar + PTZ Tracking: Radar detects movement in any weather, guiding PTZ cameras to track intruders.

Thermal Perimeters: See clearly in total darkness or through fog/smoke.

False Alarm Reduction: AI distinguishes between humans/vehicles and animals/shaking leaves.

4. Energy Management

Challenges

High energy costs reduce profit margins. Lack of visibility into which production lines are consuming the most power.

Tongfang Solution

Sub-Metering: Detailed monitoring of electricity, water, and gas per workshop.

HVAC Optimization: Smart control of ventilation in large halls to reduce waste.

Carbon Reporting: Automated reports for environmental compliance.

System Topology

Integrated Industrial IoT (IIoT) architecture.

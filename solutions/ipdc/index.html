Solution Overview

Tongfang's Data Center (IPDC) Solution provides comprehensive monitoring and control of all critical systems, ensuring maximum uptime, energy efficiency, and operational excellence for your mission-critical infrastructure.

1. Precision Cooling Systems

Challenges

Data centers generate significant heat loads that must be dissipated to maintain optimal operating temperatures for sensitive IT equipment. Inefficient cooling can lead to equipment failures, reduced lifespan, and excessive energy consumption, which accounts for up to 40% of total data center operating costs.

Traditional cooling systems often operate at fixed capacities, leading to energy waste during partial load conditions and insufficient cooling during peak loads.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides intelligent precision cooling solutions with dynamic capacity control, hot/cold aisle containment, and predictive analytics to maintain optimal temperatures while minimizing energy consumption and maximizing equipment reliability.

Dynamic Cooling Control

Variable capacity CRAC/CRAH units with real-time load matching. Automated control based on IT equipment heat output and environmental conditions.

Containment Systems

Hot aisle/cold aisle containment solutions to prevent air mixing and improve cooling efficiency. Pressure differential monitoring and control.

Benefits

  • Reduce cooling energy consumption by 30-40%
  • Maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels
  • Extend IT equipment lifespan through stable environmental conditions
  • Automatic capacity adjustment based on actual load
  • Predictive maintenance alerts for cooling equipment

Key Features

Variable Capacity Control

Automatic adjustment of cooling capacity based on real-time heat load

Airflow Management

Intelligent control of air distribution and containment systems

Predictive Analytics

AI-driven insights for cooling optimization and maintenance scheduling

Redundancy Management

Automatic failover and load sharing between cooling units

2. Power Distribution & Management

Challenges

Data centers require highly reliable and efficient power distribution systems to support critical IT loads. Power quality issues, inefficient distribution, and lack of visibility into energy consumption can lead to downtime, equipment damage, and excessive operational costs.

Traditional power monitoring systems often provide limited data, making it difficult to optimize energy usage, identify inefficiencies, or predict potential failures.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides comprehensive power management solutions with detailed monitoring, intelligent load balancing, power quality analysis, and predictive maintenance for UPS systems, PDUs, and distribution equipment.

Intelligent Power Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of power consumption at rack, row, and zone levels. Detailed analytics for capacity planning and optimization.

Power Quality Management

Continuous monitoring of voltage, frequency, harmonics, and power factor. Automatic alerts for power quality issues that could affect IT equipment.

Benefits

  • Reduce energy costs through detailed consumption tracking
  • Improve power utilization effectiveness (PUE)
  • Prevent downtime through predictive failure analysis
  • Optimize capacity planning with accurate load forecasting
  • Ensure power quality for sensitive IT equipment

Key Features

Real-time Power Monitoring

Comprehensive monitoring at all levels of power distribution

Load Balancing

Intelligent distribution of power loads across available capacity

Predictive Maintenance

Early detection of potential failures in UPS and distribution systems

Energy Analytics

Detailed reporting and analysis for energy optimization

3. Environmental Monitoring

Challenges

Data center environments must maintain precise temperature, humidity, and air quality conditions to ensure optimal equipment performance and longevity. Environmental fluctuations can lead to equipment failures, data corruption, and reduced reliability.

Traditional monitoring systems often lack the granularity and intelligence needed to proactively manage environmental conditions and prevent issues before they affect operations.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides comprehensive environmental monitoring solutions with distributed sensors, intelligent analytics, and automated control to maintain optimal conditions throughout the data center facility.

Temperature & Humidity Control

Precise monitoring and control of temperature and humidity at rack, row, and zone levels. Automated adjustments based on equipment heat loads and external conditions.

Air Quality Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of particulate matter, gases, and other air quality parameters. Early detection of contaminants that could damage sensitive equipment.

Benefits

  • Maintain optimal environmental conditions for equipment reliability
  • Early detection of potential issues before they cause downtime
  • Reduce energy consumption through intelligent environmental control
  • Comprehensive compliance reporting for environmental standards
  • Automated alerts and responses to changing conditions

4. Security & Access Control

Challenges

Data centers house critical infrastructure and sensitive information that requires robust physical security measures. Unauthorized access, security breaches, and inadequate monitoring can compromise data integrity and service availability.

Traditional security systems often operate in silos, making it difficult to correlate events, manage access permissions, and respond to security incidents effectively.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides integrated security and access control solutions that combine physical security monitoring, access management, and incident response into a unified platform for comprehensive data center protection.

Access Control Systems

Multi-factor authentication, biometric access, and role-based permissions for different areas of the data center. Complete audit trails of all access events.

Video Surveillance

High-definition video monitoring with intelligent analytics for threat detection, motion tracking, and incident investigation. Integration with access control for comprehensive security.

Benefits

  • Enhanced physical security through integrated systems
  • Granular access control with complete audit trails
  • Real-time threat detection and automated responses
  • Compliance with security standards and regulations
  • Reduced risk of unauthorized access and security breaches

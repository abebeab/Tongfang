Solution Overview

Modern data centers demand unprecedented levels of reliability, efficiency, and security. As digital infrastructure becomes increasingly critical to business operations, maintaining optimal environmental conditions, power availability, and physical security is essential for uninterrupted service delivery.

1. Precision Cooling Systems

What We Offer

Tongfang provides intelligent precision cooling solutions with dynamic capacity control, hot/cold aisle containment, and predictive analytics to maintain optimal temperatures while minimizing energy consumption.

Dynamic Cooling Control

Variable capacity CRAC/CRAH units with real-time load matching. Automated control based on IT equipment heat output.

Containment Systems

Hot aisle/cold aisle containment solutions to prevent air mixing and improve cooling efficiency.

Benefits

  • Reduce cooling energy consumption by 30-40%
  • Maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels
  • Extend IT equipment lifespan
  • Predictive maintenance alerts

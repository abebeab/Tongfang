1. Underground Parking Guidance

Challenges

Morning rush hour congestion and wasted energy on lighting in empty areas.

Tongfang Solution

Smart Guidance: Real-time vacancy tracking and LED directional arrows.

CO Linkage: Ventilation fans activate only based on air quality sensors.

2. Secure Lobby & Entry

Challenges

Slow manual visitor registration and high unauthorized access risks.

Tongfang Solution

Speed Gates: Facial recognition gates for seamless, hands-free entry.

Elevator Call: Automatic elevator dispatching to the resident's home floor.

3. High-Efficiency Office Areas

Challenges

Poor lighting conditions and over-cooling of unoccupied office zones.

Tongfang Solution

VAV Control: Dynamic airflow adjustment based on occupancy and CO2.

Adaptive Lighting: Daylight harvesting dims lights near windows automatically.

4. Intelligent Meeting Rooms

Challenges

Unused rooms booked but not occupied ("Ghost meetings") and manual curtain control.

Tongfang Solution

Smart Scenes: "One-touch" presentation mode handles blinds, lights, and AC.

Room Release: Auto-release room booking if no presence is detected.

5. Control Room

Challenges

Managing silos of data from separate security and building systems.

Tongfang Solution

Unified Dashboard: Real-time energy monitoring and alarm management in one UI.

Remote Management: Multi-building control through centralized BMS 5.0.

Solution Overview

Nowadays, parking lot operators are striving to make the most economical use of their lots. Drivers have difficulty finding a free space conveniently in enormous parking lots. Even car park operators often complain about the low utilization of spaces.

Tongfang's Parking Lot Management Solutions can guide drivers to a free space quickly, and streamline the flow of parking, freeing up spaces more quickly for further occupation, helping optimize operation, and providing extra layers of security.

1. Entrance & Exit Management

Challenges

Entrances and exits are always a top concern, especially when there are time requirements and labor costs involved. Intelligent vehicle authentication is highly required for enhanced security and smooth passage experience, such as block-list alarm, ANPR detection, etc.

Another common need is record keeping – how to quickly locate vehicles of interest to provide evidence and resolve disputes effectively.

What We Offer

Tongfang offers smooth entry and exit experience with authorized license plates to boost access efficiency. With ANPR-empowered products, vehicle records are easy to retrieve and manage for security and dispute resolution purposes.

Vehicle Records

Comprehensive recording of vehicle license plates, images, and video evidence. Advanced ANPR license plate fuzzy match, event filtering by date & time, and intelligent search capabilities.

Access Authentication

Advanced authentication systems with blocklist alarm functionality. Real-time monitoring and automatic alerts for unauthorized vehicles trying to access restricted areas.

Benefits

  • Simplify the vehicles records and retrieval for efficient security management
  • Visualize data analysis of vehicles to explore more operation insights
  • Reduce processing time by 60% during peak hours
  • Minimize human error in vehicle identification
  • Improve overall security with automated monitoring

Key Features

Recording & Documentation

Recording of vehicle license plates, images, and video evidence for all entry/exit events

ANPR Search

ANPR license plate fuzzy match, event filter by date & time, and intelligent search capabilities

Security Alerts

Blocklist alarm system for immediate notification of unauthorized vehicle access attempts

Analytics

Vehicle counting for traffic analysis and operational optimization

2. Indoor Parking Guidance

Challenges

Drivers often waste valuable time circling parking lots searching for available spots. In large multi-level parking facilities, navigation becomes stressful and confusing, leading to inefficient space utilization and poor user experience.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides intelligent parking guidance systems with real-time space availability, visual indicators, and mobile app integration to help drivers find parking spots quickly and efficiently.

Real-time Guidance

Multi-color LED indicators show spot status (Vacant/Occupied/Reserved) in real-time. Digital signage displays availability counts at each zone and level.

Vehicle Location

"Find My Car" feature through self-service kiosks or mobile app. Users can locate their vehicles using license plate entry or QR code scanning.

Benefits

  • Reduce time spent searching for parking by up to 70%
  • Increase parking space utilization by 20-30%
  • Improve customer satisfaction and experience
  • Reduce traffic congestion within parking facilities
  • Provide valuable data for facility optimization

3. Parking Fee Rules & Management

Challenges

Most parking lots face serious efficiency challenges during rush hours. Wrongful charges and fraud attempts slow down operations and result in revenue loss – often due to the lack of systematic parking lot management and flexible fee configuration.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides flexible parking fee rule configuration and retrievable vehicle payment records for most fee policies, helping speed up daily operations and prevent loss with various features, such as discount and coupon management, automatic receipt display, etc.

Benefits

  • Great usability with most commonly-used fee configuration
  • Boost loss prevention and anti-fraud tactics
  • Enhanced operation with more intuitive data-driven management
  • Reduce payment processing time by 50%
  • Eliminate revenue leakage through automated auditing

4. Operation & Management Center

Challenges

Many parking facilities lack comprehensive data for utilization optimization and have no video evidence for vehicle damage disputes. Weak operational insights without centralized reporting make it difficult to monitor multiple systems effectively.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides a unified dashboard with real-time statistics on vehicle flow and space utilization. High-definition video evidence is linked to every entry/exit event for comprehensive dispute resolution and security monitoring.

Unified Dashboard

Centralized control interface showing real-time occupancy, revenue data, and system health. Predictive analytics for capacity planning and maintenance scheduling.

Video Evidence

Complete video recording of all parking events with searchable timestamps. Automatic linking of video evidence to specific vehicle entries/exits for dispute resolution.

Benefits

  • Complete visibility into parking operations through a single interface
  • Quick resolution of disputes with comprehensive video evidence
  • Data-driven decision making with real-time analytics
  • Reduced operational costs through automated monitoring
  • Improved security with 24/7 surveillance integration

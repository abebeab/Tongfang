1. Vehicle Entrance & Exit

Challenges
  • Balancing security with resident convenience
  • Traditional keys are easily lost or duplicated
  • Visitor management is manual and unsafe
Tongfang Solution

Hands-free Access: Automated ANPR (License Plate Recognition) allows non-stop entry.

Fuzzy Search: Retrieve vehicle records instantly using partial plate numbers.

Block-list Alarm: Automatic alerts for unauthorized vehicles.

Key Features:
  • ANPR Camera
  • LED Display
  • Parking Software

2. Lobby Access

Challenges

Balancing security with resident convenience. Traditional keys are easily lost, and visitor management is often manual and unsafe.

Tongfang Solution

Touch-free Entry: Face recognition unlocks doors in < 0.2s.

Video Intercom: Residents can visually verify visitors before unlocking.

Elevator Linkage: Automatically calls the elevator to the resident's floor.

3. Public Indoor Space

Challenges

Blind spots in corridors and stairwells. Poor lighting conditions (backlight) affect video clarity.

Tongfang Solution

360° Coverage: Fisheye cameras eliminate blind spots in large halls.

WDR Technology: Ensures clear images even against strong window backlight.

4. Perimeter Protection

Challenges

Low light at night leads to false alarms from animals or leaves. Security guards cannot patrol everywhere.

Tongfang Solution

AI False Alarm Filter: Distinguishes humans/vehicles from animals.

Active Deterrence: Cameras flash lights and play audio warnings to intruders.

Thermal Imaging: See clearly in total darkness.

5. Control Room

Challenges

Too many separate systems (Video, Access, Alarm, HVAC) double the workload for staff.

Tongfang Solution

Unified Platform: Techcon BMS integrates Video, Access, and Energy data.

Visual Dashboard: Real-time health status of all devices.

1. Entrance & Atrium Climate Control

Challenges
  • High energy loss through automatic doors
  • Inconsistent temperatures affecting comfort
  • Poor air quality in high-traffic areas
  • Excessive lighting energy consumption
Tongfang Solution

Air Curtain Control: Synchronized air curtains with door operations.

Zone-based HVAC: Independent temperature control for different areas.

CO₂ Monitoring: Automated ventilation based on occupancy levels.

Smart Lighting: Daylight harvesting and occupancy-based control.

2. Individual Store Automation

Challenges
  • Manual HVAC operation leading to energy waste
  • Inconsistent product display lighting
  • Difficulty meeting tenant billing requirements
  • Poor after-hours security and energy management
Tongfang Solution

Tenant Sub-metering: Individual energy monitoring for accurate billing.

Display Lighting Control: Automated lighting scenes for product presentation.

After-hours Automation: Scheduled HVAC setback and lighting control.

Occupancy-based Systems: Systems adjust based on customer traffic.

3. Food Court & Restaurant Management

Challenges
  • Kitchen ventilation running continuously
  • Poor indoor air quality from cooking
  • High refrigeration energy consumption
  • Inefficient exhaust and makeup air systems
Tongfang Solution

Demand Kitchen Ventilation: Ventilation adjusts to cooking activity.

Grease Filter Monitoring: Automated alerts for filter maintenance.

Refrigeration Optimization: Temperature optimization for food safety.

Makeup Air Balancing: Proper air balance for kitchen exhaust systems.

4. Smart Parking & Logistics

Challenges
  • Inefficient parking guidance systems
  • High lighting energy in parking areas
  • Poor ventilation in enclosed parking
  • Complex loading dock management
Tongfang Solution

Smart Parking Guidance: Real-time space availability displays.

LED Lighting Control: Motion-activated lighting for energy savings.

CO Monitoring: Automated ventilation based on vehicle emissions.

Loading Dock Management: Integrated access and climate control.

5. Integrated Mall Control Center

Challenges
  • Multiple separate systems (HVAC, lighting, security)
  • Difficulty tracking overall mall performance
  • Manual tenant billing and reporting
  • Poor emergency response coordination
Tongfang Solution

Unified Dashboard: Single interface for all mall systems.

Performance Analytics: Real-time energy and occupancy tracking.

Automated Billing: Tenant sub-metering with automated reports.

Emergency Integration: Coordinated response with fire and security systems.

1. Intelligent Traffic Management

Challenges
  • Traffic congestion during peak hours
  • Inadequate emergency vehicle priority
  • Limited real-time traffic data for optimization
  • Poor coordination between intersections
Tongfang Solution

Adaptive Signal Control: AI-powered traffic lights adjust timing based on real-time flow.

Emergency Vehicle Preemption: Automatic green lights for ambulances and fire trucks.

Congestion Analytics: Predictive modeling to prevent bottlenecks before they form.

Key Technologies:
  • IoT Sensors
  • AI Analytics

2. Integrated Public Safety

Challenges

Fragmented emergency response systems, delayed incident reporting, and lack of cross-agency coordination during crises.

Tongfang Solution

Unified Emergency Platform: Integrates police, fire, and medical services on one dashboard.

Gunshot Detection: Acoustic sensors pinpoint location within 10 meters.

Smart Surveillance: AI-powered cameras detect unusual behavior and alert authorities.

3. Smart Energy Grid

Challenges

Energy wastage in public lighting, peak demand overloads, and inefficient distribution leading to higher costs and emissions.

Tongfang Solution

Adaptive Street Lighting: Lights dim when no motion detected, saving up to 60% energy.

Demand Response: Automatically shifts non-essential loads during peak hours.

Renewable Integration: Seamlessly incorporates solar and wind into the grid.

4. Intelligent Water Management

Challenges

Water leakage detection delayed by weeks, inefficient irrigation, and lack of real-time water quality monitoring.

Tongfang Solution

Leak Detection Sensors: Identify pipe bursts within 5 minutes, not 5 weeks.

Smart Irrigation: Weather-based scheduling reduces water usage by 40%.

Water Quality Monitoring: Continuous testing with instant contamination alerts.

5. City Command & Control Center

Challenges

Data silos across departments, delayed decision-making during emergencies, and inability to visualize city-wide operations.

Tongfang Solution

Unified Dashboard: Integrates data from all city systems onto a single 3D map.

Predictive Analytics: AI forecasts traffic, energy demand, and emergency incidents.

Cross-Department Coordination: Enables real-time collaboration between agencies.

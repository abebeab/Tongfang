Tongfang's Smart Port Platform integrates cutting-edge technologies to create intelligent, connected port ecosystems that enhance operational efficiency, improve security, and reduce environmental impact.

1. Terminal Automation Systems

Challenges

Container terminals face increasing pressure to handle larger volumes with faster turnaround times. Manual operations, inefficient yard management, and poor equipment utilization lead to bottlenecks, increased costs, and reduced throughput capacity.

Traditional terminal operations often rely on paper-based systems and manual coordination, resulting in errors, delays, and suboptimal resource allocation.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides comprehensive terminal automation solutions including automated stacking cranes, intelligent yard management systems, and real-time container tracking to optimize terminal operations and maximize throughput.

Automated Equipment Control

Remote-controlled and fully automated cranes, straddle carriers, and automated guided vehicles with precision positioning and collision avoidance systems.

Yard Management Systems

Intelligent software for optimal container placement, yard planning, and equipment dispatching. Real-time visibility of yard operations and predictive analytics.

Benefits

  • Increase terminal throughput by 30-40%
  • Reduce container handling costs by 25-35%
  • Improve equipment utilization and lifespan
  • Minimize human error and safety incidents
  • Enable 24/7 operations capability

Key Features

Automated Equipment Control

Precision control of cranes, straddle carriers, and AGVs

Real-time Container Tracking

RFID and GPS-based tracking of all containers

Predictive Maintenance

AI-driven maintenance scheduling for terminal equipment

Integrated Terminal OS

Unified operating system for all terminal operations

2. Vessel Traffic Management

Challenges

Ports must manage increasing vessel traffic while ensuring safe navigation, efficient berthing, and minimal waiting times. Poor traffic management leads to congestion, safety risks, and increased fuel consumption and emissions.

Traditional vessel traffic systems often lack real-time data integration, predictive analytics, and automated coordination between different stakeholders.

What We Offer

Tongfang's Vessel Traffic Management System (VTMS) integrates radar, AIS, CCTV, and meteorological data to provide comprehensive situational awareness, intelligent traffic planning, and automated berthing assistance.

Intelligent Traffic Planning

AI-powered algorithms for optimal vessel sequencing, berth allocation, and traffic flow management. Real-time adjustments based on weather, tides, and operational conditions.

Automated Berthing Systems

Precision navigation systems with laser guidance and real-time positioning for safe and efficient berthing operations. Reduced dependency on tugs and pilot boats.

Benefits

  • Reduce vessel waiting time by 40-50%
  • Improve berth utilization by 25-35%
  • Enhance navigation safety and reduce incidents
  • Optimize fuel consumption and reduce emissions
  • Real-time collaboration between vessels and port authorities

Key Features

Multi-Sensor Fusion

Integration of radar, AIS, CCTV, and meteorological sensors

Predictive Arrival Management

AI-based prediction of vessel arrivals and optimal scheduling

Collision Avoidance

Advanced algorithms for collision risk detection and prevention

Digital Twin Integration

Virtual port models for simulation and planning

3. Port Security Systems

Challenges

Ports are critical infrastructure requiring robust security measures against unauthorized access, theft, smuggling, and other security threats. Traditional security systems often operate in silos, creating vulnerabilities and inefficiencies.

Compliance with international security standards (ISPS Code) requires comprehensive security systems that many ports struggle to implement effectively.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides integrated port security solutions combining access control, video surveillance, perimeter protection, and threat detection systems into a unified security platform.

Access Control Systems

Multi-layered access control with biometric authentication, RFID, and facial recognition. Zone-based permissions and comprehensive audit trails for all personnel and vehicle movements.

Video Surveillance & Analytics

AI-powered video analytics for automatic threat detection, suspicious behavior recognition, and license plate recognition. Integration with access control and alarm systems.

Benefits

  • Comprehensive compliance with ISPS Code requirements
  • Reduced security incidents and unauthorized access
  • Automated threat detection and rapid response
  • Improved efficiency of security personnel
  • Integrated security operations center

Key Features

Perimeter Intrusion Detection

Fiber optic, radar, and thermal imaging for perimeter protection

Container Inspection Systems

Non-intrusive inspection technology for container security

Unified Security Platform

Integrated management of all security systems and data

Emergency Response Coordination

Automated emergency procedures and communication systems

4. Port Energy Management

Challenges

Ports are major energy consumers with significant environmental impact. Rising energy costs, regulatory pressures, and sustainability goals require ports to optimize energy usage and reduce emissions.

Traditional port operations often lack comprehensive energy monitoring, leading to waste, inefficiency, and difficulty in measuring environmental performance.

What We Offer

Tongfang's Port Energy Management System (PEMS) provides comprehensive energy monitoring, optimization, and sustainability solutions including shore power, renewable energy integration, and emission tracking.

Shore Power Systems

Automated shore-to-ship power connections allowing vessels to shut down engines while docked. Significant reduction in air pollution and noise in port areas.

Renewable Energy Integration

Solar, wind, and tidal energy systems integrated with port operations. Smart grid management and energy storage solutions for optimal renewable energy utilization.

Benefits

  • Reduce port energy costs by 20-30%
  • Decrease carbon emissions by 40-50%
  • Improve air quality in port communities
  • Compliance with environmental regulations
  • Enhanced sustainability reporting and metrics

Key Features

Energy Monitoring & Analytics

Comprehensive energy consumption tracking and analysis

Emission Tracking Systems

Real-time monitoring and reporting of port emissions

Smart Lighting Control

Intelligent lighting systems with motion sensors and scheduling

Waste Management Integration

Smart waste collection and recycling systems

SMART SPORTS FACILITIES

Solution Overview

Modern sports facilities require sophisticated automation systems to manage complex operations, ensure spectator safety, optimize energy consumption, and provide exceptional visitor experiences during both event days and non-event periods.

Tongfang's Sports Center Automation Solution integrates lighting, HVAC, access control, crowd management, and energy systems into a unified platform for efficient stadium and arena management.

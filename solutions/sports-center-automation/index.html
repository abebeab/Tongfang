Solution Overview

Modern sports facilities require sophisticated automation systems to manage complex operations, ensure spectator safety, optimize energy consumption, and provide exceptional visitor experiences during both event days and non-event periods.

Tongfang's Sports Center Automation Solution integrates lighting, HVAC, access control, crowd management, and energy systems into a unified platform for efficient stadium and arena management.

1. Access & Crowd Control Management

Challenges

Sports venues face unique security challenges with thousands of spectators entering and exiting within short timeframes. Managing ticket validation, preventing unauthorized access, and ensuring smooth crowd flow during peak periods requires advanced automation and real-time monitoring systems.

Traditional manual security checks create bottlenecks, while inadequate crowd monitoring can lead to safety hazards during emergency situations.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides integrated access control and crowd management solutions with automated ticket scanning, facial recognition, real-time occupancy monitoring, and emergency response coordination for enhanced safety and efficient spectator movement.

Smart Access Control

Multi-modal authentication including RFID tickets, mobile QR codes, and facial recognition. Automated turnstile control with real-time validation and anti-tailgating features.

Crowd Analytics

AI-powered video analytics for crowd density monitoring, flow optimization, and early detection of potential safety issues. Real-time occupancy tracking across all zones.

Benefits

  • Reduce entry processing time by 70% during peak hours
  • Enhance security with automated threat detection
  • Optimize crowd flow and prevent overcrowding
  • Improve emergency response with real-time evacuation guidance
  • Reduce operational staffing requirements

Key Features

Multi-Modal Authentication

Support for RFID, QR codes, facial recognition, and mobile ticketing

Real-time Monitoring

Live occupancy tracking and crowd density analytics across all areas

Emergency Integration

Automatic emergency response coordination and evacuation guidance

Anti-Tailgating

Advanced detection systems to prevent unauthorized entry attempts

2. HVAC & Air Quality Management

Challenges

Large sports facilities consume massive amounts of energy for heating, cooling, and ventilation. Maintaining optimal air quality and temperature for both athletes and spectators while minimizing energy costs requires sophisticated climate control systems.

Traditional HVAC systems operate at fixed schedules, leading to energy waste during low-occupancy periods and inadequate ventilation during peak events.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides intelligent HVAC systems with occupancy-based control, air quality monitoring, and predictive energy optimization to ensure optimal comfort while reducing energy consumption by up to 40%.

Smart Climate Control

Zone-based temperature and humidity control with occupancy sensors. Automatic adjustment based on real-time crowd density and external weather conditions.

Air Quality Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of CO2 levels, particulate matter, and ventilation efficiency. Automated adjustment of fresh air intake and filtration systems.

Benefits

  • Reduce HVAC energy consumption by 30-40%
  • Maintain optimal air quality for athlete performance
  • Ensure spectator comfort during all events
  • Automated maintenance alerts and predictive servicing
  • Compliance with indoor air quality regulations

4. Central Operations Management

Challenges

Managing a modern sports facility involves coordinating multiple disparate systems - security, HVAC, lighting, communications, and concessions. Without integrated monitoring and control, facility managers struggle to respond effectively to incidents and optimize operations.

What We Offer

Tongfang provides a unified operations center with real-time dashboards, predictive analytics, and centralized control of all building systems, enabling efficient facility management and rapid incident response.

Unified Dashboard

Centralized interface displaying real-time status of all systems, energy consumption, occupancy data, and security alerts. Customizable views for different operational roles.

Predictive Analytics

AI-driven insights for maintenance scheduling, energy optimization, and crowd prediction. Historical data analysis for continuous improvement.

Benefits

  • Single-point control for all facility systems
  • Rapid incident detection and response coordination
  • Data-driven decision making for operational optimization
  • Reduced staffing requirements through automation
  • Improved overall facility safety and efficiency
